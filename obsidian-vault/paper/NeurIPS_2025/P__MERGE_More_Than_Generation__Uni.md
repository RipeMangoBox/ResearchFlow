# More Than Generation: Unifying Generation and Depth Estimation via Text-to-Image Diffusion Models

[Code](https://h-embodvis.github.io/MERGE/)

**Topics**: [[T__Depth_Estimation]], [[T__Image_Generation]] | **Method**: [[M__MERGE]]

> [!tip] 核心洞察
> MERGE demonstrates that pre-trained text-to-image models can expand to depth estimation effortlessly through a play-and-plug framework with simple pluggable converters and a Group Reuse Mechanism, without degrading image generation ability.
