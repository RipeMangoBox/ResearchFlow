# FERNN

Flow equivariant recurrent neural networks (FERNNs) can be constructed by extending equivariant theory to one-parameter Lie subgroup flows, yielding significant improvements in training speed, length generalization, and velocity generalization over non-equivariant RNNs.

**研究领域**: Video Understanding, Time Series Forecasting
