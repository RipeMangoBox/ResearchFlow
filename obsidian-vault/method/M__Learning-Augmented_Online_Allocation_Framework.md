# Learning-Augmented Online Allocation Framework

A single d-dimensional vector of learned weights, combined with convex programming duality, yields a general learning-augmented framework that produces nearly optimal solutions for a broad class of online allocation problems including routing, scheduling, and fair allocation.

**研究领域**: Reinforcement Learning, Reasoning
