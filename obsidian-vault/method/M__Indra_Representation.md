# Indra Representation

Representations from unimodal foundation models implicitly reflect a shared relational structure underlying reality, and explicitly modeling this relational structure through V-enriched Yoneda embedding yields more robust and aligned representations without training.

**研究领域**: Cross-Modal Matching, Retrieval, Self-Supervised Learning
