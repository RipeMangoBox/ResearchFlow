# TrackingWorld

TrackingWorld achieves accurate and dense world-centric 3D tracking of almost all pixels by lifting sparse 2D tracks to dense tracks, filtering redundant tracks in overlapped regions, and optimizing camera poses with 3D back-projection.

**研究领域**: Object Tracking, 3D Reconstruction
