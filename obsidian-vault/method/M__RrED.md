# RrED

RrED achieves superior BUDA performance by rectifying reasoning errors through a two-stage diffusion-supervised strategy that decouples and fine-tunes diffusion encoders for enhanced visual-text alignment and leverages the target model's differential reasoning for self-correction.

**研究领域**: Domain Adaptation
