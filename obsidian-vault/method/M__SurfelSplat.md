# SurfelSplat

SurfelSplat achieves efficient, generalizable sparse-view surface reconstruction by using Nyquist sampling theorem-guided surfel adaptation and cross-view feature aggregation to enable accurate geometry prediction in a feed-forward framework.

**研究领域**: 3D Reconstruction
