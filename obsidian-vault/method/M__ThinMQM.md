# ThinMQM

Calibrating LRM thinking through training on synthetic, human-like thinking trajectories that mirror the human MQM evaluation workflow significantly reduces thinking budgets (~35x) while improving MT evaluation performance across different LRM scales.

**研究领域**: Machine Translation, Benchmark / Evaluation
