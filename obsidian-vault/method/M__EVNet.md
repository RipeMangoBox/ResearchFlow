# EVNet

Explicitly modeling subcortical visual processing (retina and LGN) through a novel SubcorticalBlock, combined with a V1-mimicking front-end, improves CNN robustness beyond biologically-inspired front-ends that only model V1.

**研究领域**: Classification, Adversarial Robustness
