# Stochastic Gradient Bandit (SGB) with characterized learning rate regimes

For two-armed bandits, there exists a sharp threshold in learning rate (scaling with the sub-optimality gap Δ) that separates logarithmic regret from polynomial regret regimes, and for K-armed bandits the learning rate must additionally scale inversely with K to avoid polynomial regret.
