# Pseudo-Labeling for KRR under Covariate Shift

A pseudo-labeling approach that splits source data for candidate model training and imputation model training, then selects candidates via pseudo-labels on target data, achieves minimax optimal excess risk up to a polylogarithmic factor.

**研究领域**: Domain Adaptation
