# GNN-SPAI

A GNN-based approach to construct Sparse Approximate Inverse (SPAI) preconditioners eliminates triangular solves, enabling efficient GPU parallelization while improving convergence through a statistics-based scale-invariant loss function.

**研究领域**: Reasoning, Math Reasoning
