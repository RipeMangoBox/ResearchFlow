# Scaling Law Comparison Framework for Open Language-Vision Models

Scaling law derivation enables systematic, robust comparison of language-vision learning procedures (CLIP vs. MaMMUT) and datasets across compute scales, revealing that MaMMUT exhibits stronger scaling and better sample efficiency than standard CLIP at larger compute budgets.
