# PH-Reg

Post Hoc Registers (PH-Reg) enables efficient integration of register tokens into existing pre-trained ViTs through self-distillation with test-time augmentation, without requiring labeled data or full retraining.

**研究领域**: Semantic Segmentation, Depth Estimation
